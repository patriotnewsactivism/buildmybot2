## SECTION 1: THE MISSION

### The Problem: The 5-Minute Death Zone

- **Fact:** If a business does not respond to a lead within **5 minutes**, the odds of closing that deal drop by **400%**.
- **Reality:** Most small business owners (SMBs) answer the phone 3 hours later. By then, the customer has called a competitor.
- **The Leak:** A business spending $2,000/month on Google Ads but missing calls is lighting money on fire.

### The Solution

We are an infrastructure layer. We do not just "chat." We:

1. **Capture:** Engage every visitor instantly (24/7).
2. **Qualify:** Ask the necessary questions to filter tire-kickers from buyers.
3. **Book:** Schedule appointments directly or route urgent calls to the owner.

## SECTION 2: TARGETING (THE HUNT)

### Who to Target (Green Light)

Look for high-ticket service providers where **one new customer = $500+ profit.**

- **Legal:** Personal Injury, Family Law, DUI (High anxiety, need instant answers).
- **Home Services:** HVAC, Roofing, Plumbing (Emergency urgency).
- **Medical:** MedSpas, Plastic Surgery, Dental Implants.
- **Real Estate:** High-volume brokerages.

### Who to Avoid (Red Light)

- **Restaurants:** Low margins.
- **Retail:** Transactional, low value.
- **Corporate:** Sales cycle too long.

### The "Ghost Shopper" Audit

**NEVER call cold.** Always audit first.

1. Go to their website.
2. Send a message via their contact form: "I need a quote for [Service]."
3. **Start a timer.**
4. If they take >15 minutes to reply, they are a qualified lead.
5. Log the failure.
