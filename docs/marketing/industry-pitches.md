# Industry Pitches

Use these as starting points. Keep it simple and focus on the top pain.

## Home Services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing)
- Pains: missed calls, after-hours inquiries, quote follow-up
- Use cases: booking, service FAQs, lead capture
- Suggested flow: "Problem -> Availability -> Booking"
- Pitch: "We help you capture service requests 24/7 so no lead slips. The bot answers common questions and books calls."
- CTA: "Want a start free pilot for one service page?"

## Real Estate
- Pains: slow replies to listings, unqualified leads
- Use cases: listing info, tour scheduling, lead capture
- Suggested flow: "Listing -> Schedule tour -> Collect info"
- Pitch: "Instant replies on listings and quick lead qualification without extra staff."
- CTA: "Lets pilot on your top listing page."
