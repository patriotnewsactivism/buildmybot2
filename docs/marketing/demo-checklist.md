# Demo Checklist

## Before the demo
- Confirm the goal and use case
- Gather 5 to 10 FAQs and services
- Prepare a simple lead capture form
- Load the demo site and test chat flow

## During the demo
- Ask a common question
- Show a fast, accurate response
- Capture a lead and show where it goes
- Show how easy setup is
- Close with the start free offer

## After the demo
- Send a recap and next step
- Confirm who owns setup
- Schedule the pilot launch date
