# BuildMyBot Agent Playbook

## Purpose
Help independent sales reps close new accounts using a simple, friendly, common sense approach. Focus on value, speed to launch, and reducing missed leads.

## Quick positioning
- One-liner: BuildMyBot is a white-label AI chatbot and voice agent platform that captures leads, answers questions, and automates follow-up 24/7.
- Who it helps: Local businesses and agencies that miss leads when their team is offline.
- Why now: Customers expect instant responses and businesses cannot afford slow follow-up.

## Ideal customer profile (ICP)
- Industries: Home services, real estate, automotive, healthcare, legal, hospitality, education, ecommerce.
- Company size: 1 to 200 employees, owner-led or lean teams.
- Common signals:
  - Missed calls or slow email response time
  - Website traffic but low conversion
  - No reliable lead capture after hours
  - Manual follow-up that slips

## Core outcomes to sell
- Faster response time to new inquiries
- More captured leads from existing traffic
- Consistent answers to common questions
- A clear path to booking calls or appointments
- Reduced manual follow-up

## The 8-step sales process
1. Target and research: Understand the prospect's service, pricing, and lead capture gaps.
2. First contact: Short, helpful message focused on missed leads and faster response.
3. Discovery call: Ask about lead flow, response times, and current tools.
4. Demo: Show the bot handling their most common questions and capturing leads.
5. Value summary: Tie features to the pains they described.
6. Start free offer: Reduce risk and get them moving today.
7. Close: Confirm next step and timeline, then send a simple recap.
8. Launch and review: Help them go live and measure results quickly.

## Discovery framework (simple and friendly)
Ask these in order:
- How do leads reach you today?
- What happens after hours or on weekends?
- What is your current response time for new inquiries?
- Which questions do you answer over and over?
- What would a great month of leads look like?

Listen for pain, then summarize:
"So the main issue is [pain], and you want [outcome]. If we can solve that, would you be open to a quick start free pilot?"

## Demo flow (10 to 15 minutes)
1. Show the bot on a website and start a conversation.
2. Ask one FAQ and show a fast, on-brand response.
3. Trigger lead capture and show the lead in CRM.
4. Show the handoff or follow-up message.
5. Highlight setup speed: website URL, branding, embed code.
6. Optional: play the voice agent preview.
7. Close: "Do you want this running on your site this week?"

## Start free path (how to position it)
- Frame it as a low-risk pilot with real outcomes.
- Keep scope simple: one bot, one website, one goal.
- Define success upfront: more captured leads and faster response.
- Set a short review window: 7 to 14 days.

Script:
"Lets start free with one bot focused on [goal]. We will review results in two weeks and decide on next steps."

## Pricing framing (common sense)
- Compare to missed leads, not to other software.
- Keep it simple: "It costs less than one lost lead per month."
- Avoid hard claims. Use real examples when available.

## Objections you will hear
- "We already have live chat."
  - "Great. BuildMyBot can handle common questions and capture leads after hours."
- "We do not have time to set this up."
  - "Setup is quick. We can have a basic bot live in under an hour."
- "We are not sure AI will sound right."
  - "You control the tone, and you can review every response during the pilot."
- "We need IT approval."
  - "The embed is a simple script. We can share a short security overview."

## Follow-up rules
- Always send a recap within 2 hours of a call.
- Schedule a firm next step on the call.
- Keep follow-ups short and specific.
- Share one proof point per follow-up, not a list.

## Daily workflow (simple targets)
- 20 to 40 new outreach touches
- 3 to 5 discovery calls booked
- 1 to 2 demos completed
- 1 next step closed per day

## Golden rules
- Listen more than you talk.
- Tie every feature to a stated pain.
- Use the start free offer to remove risk.
- Keep the close simple: "Want me to set this up today?"
