# BuildMyBot Partner Playbook

## Purpose
Help partner sales teams win clients and grow recurring revenue with BuildMyBot.

## Partner positioning
- You sell the outcome. BuildMyBot delivers the platform.
- You keep the relationship. We handle infrastructure.
- You can white-label the experience and pricing.

## Who this is for
- Agencies and consultants serving SMBs
- Local marketing and lead gen firms
- Sales reps building a book of business

## Program overview (high level)
- Tiered commissions from 20 to 50 percent based on active clients
- Optional whitelabel fee for an immediate 50 percent split
- Custom domain and branding supported

## Partner value props
- New recurring revenue without building software
- Faster close rates with a product that is easy to demo
- Strong retention because the bot stays on the website and keeps working

## Partner sales motion
1. Pick a niche and build a list
2. Run discovery and identify lead capture gaps
3. Demo a bot for their top questions
4. Present a simple start free pilot
5. Launch and review results in 7 to 14 days
6. Convert to a paid plan and expand

## Packaging and pricing guidance
Keep it simple. Offer 2 to 3 packages.
- Starter: One bot, basic lead capture, monthly reporting
- Growth: Multi page training, follow-up automation, CRM sync
- Pro: Voice agent, advanced analytics, white-label branding

Pricing tips:
- Price based on outcomes, not software features
- Show that one missed lead can cost more than the monthly fee
- Keep onboarding simple and fast

## Partner onboarding checklist
- Confirm brand name and domain
- Upload logo and brand colors
- Configure lead capture fields
- Create one flagship bot
- Install on your website and test lead capture
- Add one client and launch a pilot

## Co-selling playbook
- Bring a single page summary to the call
- Share a demo site with a live bot
- Emphasize speed to launch and low risk

## Retention playbook
- Review KPIs monthly
- Update the FAQ and services every quarter
- Add a second bot for another use case
- Offer voice agent upgrades when the client is ready

## Partner talk tracks
- "We help you capture leads 24/7 without hiring more staff."
- "We can have a live pilot on your site in days, not weeks."
- "You keep control of the brand and the pricing."

## Partner objections and answers
- "We already use a chatbot."
  - "Great. BuildMyBot can replace or complement it with lead capture and follow-up."
- "We are not technical."
  - "Setup is no-code. We handle the hard parts."
- "We need proof."
  - "Lets run a short pilot and review real results."
