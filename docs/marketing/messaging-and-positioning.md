# Messaging and Positioning

## Core message
BuildMyBot helps businesses capture more leads and respond faster with AI chatbots and voice agents that work 24/7.

## Short tagline options
- Never miss another lead
- Instant responses, real results
- Your AI sales and support team

## Elevator pitch (30 seconds)
BuildMyBot is a white-label AI chatbot and voice agent platform. It answers common questions, captures leads, and follows up automatically so businesses respond faster and convert more of their website traffic.

## Two-minute pitch
Most businesses lose leads because they respond too slowly or miss after-hours inquiries. BuildMyBot fixes that with AI chatbots and voice agents that work 24/7. It learns from your website and FAQs, keeps your brand voice, and captures leads directly into a CRM. Setup is no-code and fast, so you can start with a simple pilot and scale as results grow.

## Problem, solution, outcome
- Problem: Missed leads and slow response times
- Solution: AI chatbots and voice agents that answer and capture leads
- Outcome: Faster responses and more qualified leads

## Feature to benefit mapping
- AI chatbot builder -> faster response and higher lead capture
- Voice agent -> handle calls after hours and reduce missed calls
- Lead capture CRM -> automatic follow-up and visibility
- Analytics -> see what customers ask and improve answers
- White-label -> partners can sell under their own brand

## Audience angles
Business owner:
- "Turn more website visits into qualified leads."
Operations manager:
- "Reduce repetitive questions and improve response time."
Agency owner:
- "Offer AI lead capture under your brand without building tech."

## Proof points (common sense)
- 24/7 availability means no missed after-hours leads
- Fast responses reduce drop-off
- Automated follow-up keeps the pipeline moving

- Avoid hard promises or specific percentage lifts
