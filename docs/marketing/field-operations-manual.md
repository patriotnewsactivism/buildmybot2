# BuildMyBot.App // Field Operations Manual

**Objective:** Close accounts by solving the "Speed to Lead" crisis for local businesses.
**Role:** Strategic Revenue Partner (Not "Salesperson").
**Mantra:** "We do not sell software. We sell the revenue they are currently losing."

## Part 1: The Product & The Problem
Do not memorize features. Memorize the math.

### The Crisis: The 5-Minute Rule
- **Fact:** If a business does not respond to a lead within 5 minutes, the odds of closing that deal drop by 400%.
- **Reality:** Most small business owners (SMBs) are on a job site, in court, or with a patient. They answer the phone 3 hours later. By then, the customer has called a competitor.
- **The Leak:** A business spending $2,000/month on Google Ads but missing calls is literally lighting money on fire.

### The Solution: BuildMyBot.App
We are an infrastructure layer. We do not just "chat." We:

- **Capture:** Engage every visitor instantly (24/7).
- **Qualify:** Ask the necessary questions to filter tire-kickers from buyers.
- **Book:** Schedule appointments directly or route urgent calls to the owner.

## Part 2: Target Selection (The ICP)
Do not spray and pray. Hunt with a rifle.

### The "Green Light" Targets (High Priority)
Look for businesses where one new customer = $500+ profit.

- **Home Services:** HVAC, Roofing, Plumbing, Solar. (High emergency urgency).
- **Legal:** Personal Injury, DUI, Family Law. (High anxiety, need instant answers).
- **Medical/Esthetics:** MedSpas, Plastic Surgery, Dental Implants. (High value).
- **Real Estate:** High-volume brokerages.

### The "Red Light" Targets (Avoid)
- **Restaurants/Bars:** Margins are too low; they need reservations, not leads.
- **Retail:** Unless high-end (jewelry/furniture), the sales cycle is too short for a bot.
- **Corporate Giants:** Sales cycle takes too long. Stick to decision-makers you can talk to today.

### The "Ghost Shopper" Audit (Your Secret Weapon)
Never call a prospect cold. Do this first:

1. Go to their website.
2. Find the "Contact Us" form or chat widget.
3. Send a message: "I need a quote for [Service]. Are you available?"
4. Start a timer.
5. If they do not reply in 10 minutes, they are a qualified lead. You now have evidence of their failure.

## Part 3: The Outreach Framework
Your goal is not to check in. It is to expose a problem.

### Channel A: The Cold Call (Direct)
**Opener:** "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with BuildMyBot.App. I'm not calling to ask how you are - I'm calling because I just tested your website's contact form 15 minutes ago, and nobody has gotten back to me yet."

**The Hook:** "If I were a real customer looking to spend money, I would have already called your competitor by now. We build AI agents that fix this delay instantly. Do you have 30 seconds to discuss how many leads you missed last week?"

### Channel B: The Email (Evidence-Based)
```text
Subject: I tested your response time (Results attached)

[Name],

I visited your site at 9:00 AM and submitted an inquiry. It is now 11:00 AM, and I haven't received a reply.

Data shows that 78% of customers buy from the business that responds first. You are currently letting those leads slip to competitors.

I can install a BuildMyBot.App agent on your site that answers, qualifies, and books these leads in under 10 seconds.

Are you open to a 5-minute demo this afternoon?

-[Your Name]
```

## Part 4: The Demo (Show, Don't Tell)
Keep the demo under 15 minutes. Use their phone, not your screen.

### Step 1: The Setup
"Pull out your cell phone. Go to this URL." (Have a demo page ready that looks like their industry.)

### Step 2: The Engagement
"Type in: 'How much do you charge?'"

Action: The bot replies instantly with the correct, branded answer.
You say: "See that? That happened instantly. No waiting for a receptionist."

### Step 3: The Capture
"Now type: 'I want to book an appointment.'"

Action: The bot asks for name/number or offers a calendar link.
You say: "The bot just did the work of a sales rep. It collected the info. You get a notification on your phone saying 'New Hot Lead.' You didn't have to lift a finger."

### Step 4: The Voice (If applicable)
"Call this number."

Action: They hear the AI Voice Agent handle the call.
You say: "This works while you sleep. It works while you are on a roof. It works while you are in court."

## Part 5: Closing & Pricing
Price based on value, not cost.

### The Math Close
```text
Prospect: "How much is it?"
You: "Before I tell you, how much is one new [Roof/Case/Patient] worth to you?"
Prospect: "$3,000."
You: "Okay. BuildMyBot.App is [Price/Month]. If the bot captures just one customer that you would have otherwise missed, the system is free for the month. Everything else is pure profit. Does that math work for you?"
```

### The "Start Free" Pilot (The Risk Remover)
If they hesitate, say:

```text
I understand you've been burned by marketing agencies before. Let's do this:

I set up the bot.
We run it for 7 days.
If it doesn't capture a lead, we turn it off and shake hands. No cost.
If it does work, we sign the contract. Fair?
```

## Part 6: Handling Objections
Be analytical. Don't argue, just correct the logic.

**"I have a receptionist."**
Response: "Does your receptionist work at 9:00 PM on a Sunday? 40% of web traffic happens after hours. BuildMyBot.App covers the shifts your staff can't."

**"I don't like AI/Robots."**
Response: "I agree. Bad bots are annoying. Ours is trained on your data to sound like you. And honestly, customers prefer a robot that answers instantly over a human they have to wait 4 hours for."

**"I need to talk to my web guy."**
Response: "Great. I will send you a single line of code. Email it to him. He can paste it in within 2 minutes. It is easier than embedding a YouTube video."

## Part 7: The Setup Checklist (Post-Sale)
Do not close the deal and disappear. Speed to launch is vital.

1. Get the data: Ask for their top 10 FAQs (pricing, hours, service area, process).
2. Get the tone: Formal (lawyer) or friendly (plumber)?
3. Get the goal: Do they want a phone call, a form fill, or a booked calendar slot?
4. Send the code: Email the snippet immediately.
5. Test live: Once installed, record a video of yourself using it and send it to the client. "We are live."
