# Objection Handling

Use a calm tone. Validate first, then reframe, then ask a question.

## 1) "We already use live chat"
Response: "Great. BuildMyBot can cover after-hours, capture leads, and answer FAQs so your team focuses on higher value chats."
Ask: "Would it help to see a pilot focused on after-hours leads?"

## 2) "We do not trust AI"
Response: "That is fair. You control the tone and can review responses during a pilot."
Ask: "If the bot stayed within your approved FAQs, would you be open to testing it?"

## 3) "We do not have time"
Response: "Setup is fast. We can start with one use case and one page."
Ask: "Can we schedule a 30 minute setup and go live this week?"

## 4) "We do not have budget"
Response: "Totally understand. Most clients start free and decide once they see lead volume."
Ask: "Would a short pilot help you decide?"

## 5) "We need IT"
Response: "The embed is a simple script. We can provide a security summary."
Ask: "Can I send that and loop them in?"

## 6) "We are a small team"
Response: "That is exactly who benefits. The bot handles repetitive questions so your team can focus."
Ask: "What is the biggest time sink in your current support?"

## 7) "Our industry is unique"
Response: "That is common. The bot trains on your content and tone."
Ask: "Can we build a pilot with your top 10 questions?"

## 8) "We get few website leads"
Response: "The bot helps convert existing traffic and captures more of what you already have."
Ask: "Would you like to measure this for two weeks?"

## 9) "We tried chatbots before"
Response: "Many older tools are rules-based. This learns from your content and improves."
Ask: "What did not work last time?"

## 10) "We only want phone calls"
Response: "You can use the voice agent or send calls to a human. It still captures leads."
Ask: "Would you like a voice pilot?"

## 11) "We are worried about compliance"
Response: "We can keep answers within your approved content and log conversations."
Ask: "What compliance rules do we need to follow?"

## 12) "Send me info"
Response: "Happy to. I will send a short summary and one example. Can we lock a 15 minute review?"
Ask: "What time works?"
